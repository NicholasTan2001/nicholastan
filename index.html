Project: Car Sales Management (Software Application)

Project Resources Link: https://github.com/NicholasTan2001/carSalesManagement.git
  The Car Sales Management System is a comprehensive Java-based application designed to streamline the buying and selling of cars. The system provides role-based login functionality, allowing users to log in as either an admin or a customer. Admins have full control over the system, with the ability to modify car details, and perform CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations on the car inventory. Customers, on the other hand, can browse through the available car details and proceed with purchases seamlessly.

 All data, including user credentials, car information, and purchase records, is securely stored and managed using a MySQL database, ensuring a reliable and organized backend structure. This project highlights practical database integration, robust role management, and user-centric functionality.

Developed by using Apache NetBeans and MySQL.
